Do not use KNULLI Gladiator II (2025-08-13) or below with brand new RG40XX H/V devices

For the latest revision of the RG40XX series, Anbernic apparently decided to go with different screens. Unfortunately, the screens of the new RG40XX H/V models are not compatible with KNULLI, yet. Any attempt to run an incompatible CFW on the new RG40XX H/V models might cause damage your screen. If you see unexpected stripes on your screen after flashing KNULLI, turn the device off immediately. We are aware of the problem and we are working on a solution.

Since our team was not supplied with the new RG40XX H/V models, we are working closely with affected users within our community to resolve the issue. We already have a promising solution in alpha testing.

For now, we ask you to please be patient - help is on the way.
