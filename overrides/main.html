KNULLI Gladiator II (2025/12/22) supports new screens in RG40XX H/V devices!

Good news everyone! We have updated the KNULLI Gladiator II builds for the RG40XX H/V (you can tell by the date of the newer builds being 2025/12/22) to be compatible with the new screen model that's used in the Anbernic RG40XX H/V devices.

Simply download the new build and flash it to your primary SD card as explained on our website. The build is compatible with both, the old and the new model. However, if your device has the old screen, you don't need this update.
