# :material-palette-outline: Customization

KNULLI comes with a lot of options to customize your gaming experience and make your KNULLI-driven device truly your own.

* You can **customize** the default KNULLI theme (including its **Splash screen**) or install different themes as explained in the [Themes](./themes.md) section
* You can add your own **background music** as explained in the [Background Music](./background-music.md) section
* You can install your preferred **bezel decorations** as explained in the [Bezel Decorations](./bezel-decorations.md) section
* You can replace the **boot logo** with a different image as explained in the [Boot Logo](./bootlogo.md) section
* You can add your own **shaders** and make them available directly in Knulli as explained in the [Shaders](./shaders.md) section
