# :material-monitor: Display Settings

With KNULLI Scarab we introduced a *Display Settings* menu where you can adjust a variety of details about your build in screen.

You will find the *Display Settings* settings in the *Device Settings* menu.

* Press ++"Start"++ to bring up the main menu
* Go to *Device Settings*
* Go to *Display Settings*

!!! info "Not every device supports (all) display settings"

    It depends entirely on the device whether the menu even shows up (only if at least one setting is available to you) and if so, which settings will be presented to you. Do not be alarmed if the menu does not show on your device or seems incomplete - your device might simply not support all display settings.

## Available settings

* **Temperature** lets you adjust the color temperature (default value is 50). High values result in a reddish screen while low values lead to a blueish screen.
