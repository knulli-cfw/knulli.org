## Installation

The Retroid Pocket Flip 2 is capable of booting both Android **and** Linux.
To run KNULLI on your Retroid device, simply prepare a KNULLI SD card as explained in our [Quick Start Guide](../../play/quick-start.md) and plug it in the SD Card slot of your Retroid device, then follow these steps:

* Shut down your Retroid device (or reboot it from the Android menu).
* In both cases, make sure to **press and hold** ++"Volume Up"++ when the device is turning on again!
* If you did it right, you will see a very simple text-based menu (might appear sideways).
* The menu can be navigated by pressing ++"Volume Up"++ and ++"Volume Down"++.
* Pick the option *Boot* and tap ++"Power"++ to confirm.
* The device should now boot KNULLI from your SD card!
